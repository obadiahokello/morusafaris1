Safari Symphony: An East African Adventure (12 Days) - Starting from $10,000 USD per person

Detailed Itinerary

This 12-day "Safari Symphony" adventure is designed to immerse you in the breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and rich cultures of East Africa. You'll explore iconic destinations like the Maasai Mara National Reserve, Serengeti National Park, Ngorongoro Crater, Amboseli National Park, Lake Manyara National Park, Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, and Volcanoes National Park. The highlight of the itinerary is undoubtedly the unforgettable experience of trekking through lush rainforests and observing mountain gorillas in their natural habitat.

Day 1: Arrival in Nairobi, Kenya

.

  • Morning: Arrive in Nairobi, Kenya, and check into your hotel.
  • Afternoon: Choose between two exciting options:
  • Visit the Giraffe Centre and hand-feed these gentle giants. Afterwards, head to the Karen Blixen Museum for a glimpse into Kenya's colonial history and the inspiration behind "Out of Africa."
  • Embark on a thrilling afternoon game drive in Nairobi National Park, spotting lions, zebras, giraffes, and other wildlife within sight of the city skyline.
  • Evening: Enjoy a traditional Kenyan dinner with vibrant live music and captivating dance performances, immersing yourself in the local culture.
Day 2: Nairobi to Maasai Mara National Reserve, Kenya (Flight)

  • Morning: Enjoy a relaxed breakfast at your Nairobi hotel.
  • Afternoon: Transfer to Wilson Airport (WIL) for your short domestic flight to Maasai Mara National Reserve. Upon arrival, connect with a ground transfer to your lodge.
  • Rest of the Day: Settle into your lodge, enjoy a delicious welcome meal, and relax amidst the stunning scenery of the Mara. In the evening, embark on your first exciting game drive, searching for the Big Five under the African sunset.
Day 3 & 4: Maasai Mara National Reserve, Kenya

  • Dedicate these two full days to exploring the vast plains of the Maasai Mara. Enjoy thrilling morning and afternoon game drives, searching for a diverse range of wildlife, including lions, leopards, elephants, zebras, giraffes, and wildebeest (depending on the season). Witness the breathtaking spectacle of the Great Migration if you're visiting during the peak months (July-October).
  • Evenings: Relax at your lodge, indulge in delicious meals, and enjoy captivating cultural performances showcasing Maasai traditions.
Day 5: Maasai Mara to Serengeti National Park, Tanzania (Flight)

  • Morning: Enjoy a leisurely breakfast at your lodge in the Maasai Mara.
  • Afternoon: Take a scenic domestic flight from Mara airstrip (Mara Serena or Keekorok) to Serengeti National Park's airstrip (Seronera or Kogatende). Upon arrival, connect with a ground transfer to your luxurious tented camp nestled within the Serengeti.
  • Rest of the Day: Settle into your camp, take in the breathtaking views of the savannah, and enjoy a delightful dinner under the star-studded African sky.
Day 6 & 7: Serengeti National Park & Ngorongoro Crater, Tanzania

  • Spend two full days exploring the vast expanse of Serengeti National Park, Tanzania's crown jewel. Embark on exciting morning and afternoon game drives, spotting a diverse range of wildlife, including lions, cheetahs, elephants, zebras, wildebeest, and many more.
  • On Day 7: Depart Serengeti National Park and head towards the breathtaking Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Descend into the crater rim for an unforgettable afternoon game drive, observing wildlife concentrated within this "natural zoo."
  • Evenings: Enjoy delicious meals and relaxation at your Serengeti camp or lodge on Day 6. On Day 7, stay overnight on the rim of the crater, offering stunning views and a unique experience.
Day 8: Ngorongoro Crater to Amboseli National Park, Kenya (Flight)

  • Morning: Embark on a final game drive within the Ngorongoro Crater, searching for any wildlife you might have missed the previous day.
  • Afternoon: Ascend back to the rim of the crater and take a short domestic flight from a local airstrip (Ndutu or Lobo) to Amboseli National Park's airstrip (Amboseli). Upon arrival, connect with a ground transfer to your lodge.
  • Rest of the Day: Settle into your lodge, surrounded by the majestic scenery of Amboseli, and enjoy a delightful dinner.
Day 9: Amboseli National Park & Lake Manyara National Park, Tanzania (Flight)

  • Morning: Enjoy a breathtaking morning game drive in Amboseli, seeking out elephants and other wildlife with the majestic Kilimanjaro as a backdrop.
  • Afternoon: Take a domestic flight from Amboseli airstrip to Lake Manyara National Park's airstrip. Upon arrival, embark on an afternoon game drive around the lake, spotting unique species like tree-climbing lions.
  • Rest of the Day: Settle into your lodge near Lake Manyara, offering stunning views and a relaxing atmosphere.
Day 10: Lake Manyara to Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, Uganda (Flight)

  • Morning: Explore Lake Manyara National Park on a morning game drive, or choose an optional activity like birdwatching or a cultural tour delving into the local way of life.
  • Afternoon: Transfer to a local airstrip and board a domestic flight to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park in Uganda, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and home to half of the world's mountain gorillas.
  • Rest of the Day: Upon arrival, check into your lodge nestled within the lush rainforest, a perfect base for your upcoming gorilla encounter. In the evening, enjoy a delicious dinner and relax, preparing for your exciting trek tomorrow.
Day 11: Gorilla Trekking in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, Uganda

  • Morning: Embark on the most anticipated experience of your journey - gorilla trekking in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest. Spend an unforgettable morning tracking and observing a habituated gorilla family in their natural habitat, learning about their social behavior and importance to the ecosystem.
  • Afternoon: After your trek, return to the lodge for a relaxing lunch and time to reflect on your incredible encounter with these majestic primates.
  • Evening: Enjoy a celebratory dinner at your lodge, sharing stories and experiences with your fellow travelers.
Day 12: Bwindi Impenetrable Forest to Kigali & Departure (Flight)

  • Morning: Savor a leisurely breakfast at your lodge and enjoy the beautiful rainforest surroundings.
  • Afternoon: Transfer to a local airstrip and board a domestic flight to Kigali International Airport (KGL) in Rwanda. Depending on your departure flight schedule, you may have some time for relaxation or souvenir shopping at the airport.
  • Rest of the Day: Bid farewell to East Africa with a heart full of cherished memories as you board your international flight home.
Inclusions:

  • Accommodation in comfortable lodges and tented camps throughout the safari
  • Meals as per the itinerary (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)
  • Local flights between destinations as mentioned in the itinerary
  • Ground transportation in safari vehicles
  • Park entrance fees for all national parks and reserves mentioned
  • Services of professional and experienced guides throughout the safari
  • Gorilla trekking permits in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest and Volcanoes National Park (subject to availability)
  • Bottled water throughout the safari
Exclusions:

  • International flights to and from Nairobi, Kenya
  • Visa fees for Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, and Uganda (if applicable)
  • Travel insurance (highly recommended)
  • Drinks and beverages other than what's included in the meals
  • Gratuities for guides, lodge staff, and porters (at your discretion)
  • Optional activities not mentioned in the itinerary
Price:

  • Starting from $10,000 USD per person (based on double occupancy). Please note that prices may vary depending on travel dates, flight availability, and lodge upgrades.
Please note:

  • This itinerary is a suggestion and can be customized to fit your interests, preferences, and budget.
  • The best time to visit East Africa for a safari is during the dry season (June to October), when wildlife viewing is at its peak. However, the Great Migration is best witnessed from July to October
  • Depending on the season and availability, alternative accommodation options may be offered.
  • Gorilla trekking permits are limited and need to be booked well in advance
