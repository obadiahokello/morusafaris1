Whispers of the Wild: A Ugandan & Rwandan Honeymoon (10 Days) - Starting from $7,000 USD per person

Embrace the untamed beauty of East Africa on this romantic 10-day honeymoon, venturing into the lush rainforests of Uganda and Rwanda in search of gorillas and exploring breathtaking landscapes.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Entebbe, Uganda

  • Morning: Arrive at Entebbe International Airport and complete immigration procedures.
  • Afternoon: Transfer to your charming hotel in Entebbe and unwind after your flight. In the evening, enjoy a delicious welcome dinner with breathtaking views of Lake Victoria.
Day 2: Jinja & The Source of the Nile

  • Morning: Embark on a scenic drive to Jinja, the adventure capital of Uganda.
  • Afternoon: Visit the source of the mighty Nile River, the longest river in Africa and the cradle of ancient civilizations. Take a boat cruise on the Nile, soaking in the beauty of the surrounding landscapes.
  • Optional Activities:
  • White-water rafting (additional cost): For a thrilling experience, embark on a white-water rafting adventure on the Nile River, navigating exciting rapids and enjoying the adrenaline rush.
  • Visit local markets: Immerse yourselves in the vibrant atmosphere of Jinja's local markets, browsing for unique souvenirs and handcrafted items.
Day 3: Murchison Falls National Park

  • Morning: Depart Jinja and embark on a scenic drive to Murchison Falls National Park, Uganda's largest protected wildlife area.
  • Afternoon: Enjoy an afternoon game drive on the vast savannas, searching for elephants, lions, giraffes, and a diverse range of other wildlife. Witness the powerful Murchison Falls, where the Nile River plunges over a 43-meter drop.
Day 4: Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

  • Morning: After breakfast, depart Murchison Falls National Park and drive through rolling hills and verdant landscapes towards Bwindi Impenetrable National Park.
  • Afternoon: Nestled within a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Bwindi Impenetrable National Park is home to half of the world's remaining mountain gorillas. Relax at your lodge nestled amidst the rainforest, enjoying the serenity of nature.
Day 5: Gorilla Trekking in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

  • Morning: Embark on the most unforgettable experience of your honeymoon - gorilla trekking in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park. Spend an incredible morning tracking a habituated gorilla family in their natural habitat, observing their social interactions and gentle nature.
  • Afternoon: After your trek, return to the lodge and share stories of your incredible encounter with these majestic primates. Enjoy a romantic candlelit dinner under the starlit African sky.
Day 6: Lake Mutanda & Mgahinga Gorilla National Park

  • Morning: Depart Bwindi and journey towards Lake Mutanda, a beautiful crater lake nestled within the Virunga Mountains. Enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes.
  • Afternoon: Take a scenic boat cruise on the lake, with opportunities to spot hippos, crocodiles, and a variety of birdlife. In the afternoon, continue your journey to Mgahinga Gorilla National Park, bordering Volcanoes National Park in Rwanda.
Day 7: Golden Monkey Tracking in Mgahinga Gorilla National Park

  • Morning: Embark on a golden monkey tracking adventure in Mgahinga Gorilla National Park's bamboo forests. Observe these playful primates with their distinctive golden fur as they swing through the trees.
  • Afternoon: Enjoy a romantic picnic lunch in a scenic location within the park, surrounded by the beauty of the Virunga Mountains. Relax at your lodge and soak in the tranquility of your surroundings.
Day 8: Kigali, Rwanda

.

  • Morning: Cross the border into Rwanda and explore the vibrant capital city, Kigali.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Kigali Genocide Memorial, a poignant reminder of the country's dark past. Immerse yourselves in Rwanda's rich culture and art scene by browsing local markets and visiting museums.
  • Evening: Enjoy a romantic dinner at a rooftop restaurant, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the city.
Day 9: Volcanoes National Park, Rwanda

  • Morning: Travel to Volcanoes National Park, a haven for mountain gorillas and other primates.
  • Afternoon: Choose between another unforgettable gorilla trekking experience or embark on a scenic hike to witness the breathtaking scenery of the Virunga Volcanoes, including Mount Karisimbi, the highest mountain in Rwanda.
Day 10: Departure

  • Morning: Savor a leisurely breakfast at your lodge and enjoy the breathtaking views of the Virunga Volcanoes.
  • Afternoon: Transfer to Kigali International Airport for your departure flight home, carrying cherished memories of your Ugandan and Rwandan honeymoon adventure.

  • Services of professional and experienced guides throughout the safari

Price:

  • This itinerary is a suggestion and can be customized to fit your interests, preferences, and budget.
