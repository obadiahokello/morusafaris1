Heart of the Pearl: An Unforgettable Ugandan Adventure

Experience the diverse beauty and rich heritage of Uganda, known as the "Pearl of Africa". Encounter majestic mountain gorillas, discover mesmerizing landscapes, embark on thrilling safaris, and immerse yourself in the vibrant local culture. This journey will leave you with lifelong memories.

Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty and diverse wildlife of Uganda, the Pearl of Africa, on this unforgettable 7-day adventure. Explore iconic national parks, trek through lush rainforests, encounter majestic primates, and discover the rich culture of this vibrant East African nation.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Entebbe & Jinja

  • Morning: Arrive at Entebbe International Airport and complete immigration procedures.
  • Afternoon: Transfer to your comfortable hotel in Entebbe, overlooking the shores of Lake Victoria, Uganda's largest lake. In the afternoon, depart for Jinja, the adventure capital of Uganda.
Day 2: The Source of the Nile & Optional White-Water Rafting

  • Morning: Explore the source of the mighty Nile River, the longest river in Africa, and learn about its historical significance. Take a boat cruise on the Nile, soaking in the beauty of the surrounding landscapes.
  • Afternoon: Choose between an optional thrilling white-water rafting adventure on the Nile or a visit to the vibrant local markets in Jinja, browsing for unique souvenirs and handcrafted items.
Day 3: Murchison Falls National Park

  • Morning: Depart Jinja and embark on a scenic drive to Murchison Falls National Park, Uganda's largest protected wildlife area.
  • Afternoon: Enjoy an afternoon game drive on the vast savannas, searching for elephants, lions, giraffes, and a diverse range of other wildlife. Witness the powerful Murchison Falls, where the Nile River plunges over a 43-meter drop.
Day 4: Chimpanzee Trekking in Budongo Forest Reserve

  • Morning: After breakfast, depart Murchison Falls National Park and drive towards Budongo Forest Reserve, a lush rainforest teeming with diverse wildlife.
  • Afternoon: Embark on an unforgettable chimpanzee trekking adventure in Budongo Forest Reserve. Track a habituated chimpanzee community in their natural habitat and observe their fascinating social behavior.
Day 5: Queen Elizabeth National Park

  • Morning: Depart Budongo Forest Reserve and embark on a scenic drive to Queen Elizabeth National Park, known for its diverse ecosystems and stunning landscapes.
  • Afternoon: Enjoy an afternoon boat cruise on the Kazinga Channel, a natural channel connecting Lakes George and Edward. Spot a variety of wildlife, including hippos, crocodiles, and a diverse array of birdlife.
Day 6: Game Drives in Queen Elizabeth National Park

  • Morning: Embark on an exciting morning game drive in Queen Elizabeth National Park, searching for lions, elephants, buffaloes, and a variety of other wildlife. Explore the Ishasha sector, famous for its tree-climbing lions.
  • Afternoon: Enjoy another game drive in a different area of the park, maximizing your chances of spotting a wider range of wildlife. In the evening, relax at your lodge and share stories of your Ugandan adventures.
Day 7: Entebbe & Departure

  • Morning: Savor a leisurely breakfast at your lodge in Queen Elizabeth National Park, enjoying the beautiful scenery.
  • Afternoon: Transfer to Entebbe for your departure flight home, carrying cherished memories of your Ugandan adventure.
